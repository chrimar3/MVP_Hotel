🏨 Hotel Review Generator - Demo

This page demonstrates different URL routing scenarios for the Hotel Review Generator. Each link shows how the system adapts based on the booking source.

📧 Direct/Walk-in Guests

For guests who booked directly or walked in:

?source=direct or no parameter

Expected behavior: Primary platform = TripAdvisor, Secondary = Google Maps

🏨 Direct Booking Demo

🏨 Booking.com Guests

For guests who booked through Booking.com:

?source=booking

Expected behavior: Primary platform = Booking.com, Secondary = TripAdvisor + Google Maps

🏨 Booking.com Demo

✈️ Expedia Guests

For guests who booked through Expedia:

?source=expedia

Expected behavior: Primary platform = Expedia, Secondary = TripAdvisor + Google Maps

✈️ Expedia Demo

🔧 Invalid Source (Fallback)

For invalid or unknown sources:

?source=invalid

Expected behavior: Falls back to direct booking behavior
