🧠 Psychological Principles Applied

  • Loss Aversion: Progress bar shows what users will lose if they abandon
  • Endowment Effect: Points earned create ownership feeling
  • Social Proof: Live ticker validates participation
  • Variable Rewards: Different achievements at unpredictable intervals
  • Commitment Consistency: Small initial action (star rating) leads to completion
  • Peak-End Rule: Celebration at completion creates positive memory
